Website for Local Business

  • Skills: Technical Consulting, UI/UX, E-Commerce

A website for local Business 'The Crib'. This website is an e-commerce store which serves hundreds of weekly customers.

The theme of the website is colourful and comfy, just like the product. What really ties it together is the product photography, the perfectly white background that let's the product just float on the page. This stands out particularly in the 4th image ,the shop page, where every variety of 'Cribs' are listed.

The website is implemented with Wordpress and detailed with HTML. The hosting service used is BlacnKnight Furthermore, functional wordpress plugins are altered in PHP.

The WordPress plugins WooCommerce and Stripe payment gateway alleviate much of the difficulty of the payment process and makes the store way more credible and trustworthy.
