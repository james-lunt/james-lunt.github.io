Website for Local Football Club

  • Category: Web Development
  • Technologies: WordPress, PHP, HTML, Siteground
  • Skills: Technical Consulting, UI/UX
  • Link: blackrockcollegeafc.com

blackrockcollegeafc.com is a website designed for local sports club Blackrock College AFC. Established in 1983, the club was overdue a central webpage to help attribute its consistent social media campaign.

For the clubs 3 teams the website provides updates to players on fixtures, results, club ongoings and scoring leaderboards as well as pointers to competing leagues.

Externally, the website helps attract new players, simply locates the home sports grounds and enables contact to the clubs administration with ease.

The website was designed with the efforts of the clubs committee members to create something meaningful that represents a competitive sports team and its community.

The colour pallette is aligned with the clubs kit colours. And images throughout the website that include players, squads, events & sports grounds give realism and integrity to the page.

The website is implemented with Wordpress and detailed with HTML. The hosting service used is Siteground Furthermore, functional wordpress plugins are altered in PHP.
